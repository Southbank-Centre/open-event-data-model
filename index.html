What is the Open Event Data Model?

The Open Event Data Model (OEDM) is an open standard for describing cultural events, including associated people, places and creative works. an standard for sharing cultural event data between systems and users.Read more about the OEDM schemas.

In addition to publishing the model on these pages, we are also encouraging the development of a range of tools that are built around the OEDM. Find out about other projects related to OEDM.

How to get involved

The OEDM is maintained by the community of users, including Southbank Centre and the Barbican. If you are interested in getting involved or contributing, contact us at open-event@southbankcentre.co.uk.
